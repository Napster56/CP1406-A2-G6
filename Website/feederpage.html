Music concerts and events in North Queensland

Australian Festival Chamber of Music (28 July – 5 August)

A well-established event in Townsville with an international reputation. Classical musicians from Australia and around the world will be performing.

Some of the local players who will be featured this year are members of the Barrier Reef Orchestra which is located in Townsville.

Arthur Athan will be playing Beethoven's Piano Concerto No.5, and has previously played with the Barrier Reef Orchestra.

Townsville - QSO Plays Sibelius

Under the baton of Raymond Yong the Orchestra will indulge audiences with Dvorák’s splendidly vibrant Carnival Overture, alongside one of Sibelius’ most popular works, Symphony No.2. Esteemed violinist Kristian Winther will light up the stage with Mendelssohn’s beloved and joyful Violin Concerto in E minor.
