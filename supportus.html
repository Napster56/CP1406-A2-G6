Become a Benefactor

You are invited to become an Orchestra Benefactor and assist Townsville’s Community Orchestra with your personal and publicly acknowledged philanthropy, which encourages others to do the same.

You can become an Orchestra Benefactor with a philanthropic gift of $200 or more.

Benefits of becoming a Benefactor

  • Benefactors are placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months.
  • The Benefactor roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months.
  • As a benefactor you will also receive two complimentary tickets to our next concert.
  • Benefactors will be invited as guests at our annual Season’s Launch function.
  • All Benefactors will receive our newsletter.
  • The Barrier Reef Orchestra is registered as a Deductible Gift Recipient and donations are tax deductible.

For more information contact Simon McConnell 0418 776961, brotownsville@gmail.com.

Become a Member

You can support the Barrier Reef Orchestra by becoming an Orchestra Member. Your subscription helps to keep us operating and we provide substantial discounts whenever possible.

Benefits of becoming a Member

  • Ticket discounts of up to 50% apply to Members who attend any events of the Barrier Reef Orchestra
  • The Barrier Reef Orchestra is registered as a Deductible Gift Recipient and any additional donations are tax-deductible!
